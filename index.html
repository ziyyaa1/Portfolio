Hi, I am Ziya & I build stuff

I kicked things off at FAST Karachi, and now I’m majoring in Computer Science at the University of Manitoba — casually stacking minors in Math, Stats, and Econ along the way (because apparently I enjoy stress).

I like building things that actually work (and sometimes break, but we move). From full stack apps to AI/ML, crypto, and blockchain — I’m into tech that’s shaping what’s next, not just what’s hyped right now.
